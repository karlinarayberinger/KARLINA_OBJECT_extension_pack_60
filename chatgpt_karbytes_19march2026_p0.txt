So instead of:

> adrenaline = insecurity

A better framing:

> adrenaline = “high alert / readiness to respond”

That response can come from:

Fear (danger)

Excitement (roller coaster, competition)

Urgency (deadline, performance)

👉 It’s not inherently negative—it’s about intensity and activation.
