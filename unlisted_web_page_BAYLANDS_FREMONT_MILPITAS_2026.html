BAYLANDS_FREMONT_MILPITAS_2026

The following collection of photographs and videos depicts the baylands which span the south-east end of the (California) San Francisco Bay Area bay (south of the Dumbarton Bridge (connecting the city of Newark on the east side to Menlo Park on the west side via a walkable stretch of roadway crossing the bay). The aforementioned baylands region comprises the Don Edwards Wildlife preserve and encompasses the marshes and wetland bordering Newby Island and the Alviso marina area. Most of the photographs and videos on this web page feature the trail through Mallard Slough (which was closed until this year) and which is on the south side of Coyote creek such that a remnant building of the ghost town named Drawbridge is visible from the Mallard Slough trail.
